The Heart Wellness programme was developed to prevent and cure people with all types of heart disease in 9 locations in the Greater Sydney and Melbourne Regions. It is the top cardiac rehabilitation programme in Australia. With the fundamental goal of maintaining a relationship with the patient after the programme so they maintain that specialised support to continue with their new healthy lifestyle and improve their best long-term health outcomes, the Heart Wellness programme is our modern approach to cardiac rehabilitation, serving the community in both the primary and secondary prevention of heart disease. All three phases of cardiac rehabilitation programmes may be found within the Macquarie Health Group of Hospitals, and they also offer a preventative programme for people who are at risk.
